# Partnership Agreement

**Between:** Hivemind Engine Inc. ("Hivemind")
**And:** [PARTNER_NAME] ("Partner")

**Effective Date:** [DATE]

## 1. Partnership Overview

This agreement establishes a strategic partnership between Hivemind and Partner to integrate products and share customer referrals.

### 1.1 Integration

Partner will provide users with the ability to export/integrate with Hivemind's autonomous deployment platform. Hivemind will provide technical documentation and support for the integration.

### 1.2 Co-Marketing

Both parties agree to co-market the integration through:
- Joint blog posts and case studies
- Co-branded webinars (minimum 1 per quarter)
- Social media promotion
- Email newsletter features
- Conference appearances (when applicable)

### 2.1 Commission Structure

Partner will receive a **[COMMISSION_RATE]% recurring commission** on all customers referred through Partner's integration or referral links.

### 2.2 Referral Attribution

Referrals will be tracked via:
- Partner-specific referral parameters (`?ref=[PARTNER_TYPE]`)
- API integration tracking

### 2.3 Commission Calculation

Commissions are calculated based on:
- **Subscription Revenue:** Partner earns [COMMISSION_RATE]% of monthly recurring revenue (MRR)
- **Recurring Payments:** Commission continues for the lifetime of the subscription
- **Cancellations:** Commission stops when customer cancels subscription

### 2.4 Payment Terms

- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Minimum Payout:** $[MINIMUM_PAYOUT] USD
- **Payment Method:** Stripe Connect transfer to Partner's account
- **Payment Schedule:** Within 15 days of month end
- **Reporting:** Monthly commission report provided by 1st of each month

## 4. Partner Responsibilities

### 4.1 Integration

Partner agrees to:
- Maintain integration functionality
- Provide clear documentation to users
- Support users experiencing integration issues
- Notify Hivemind of integration changes 30 days in advance

### 4.2 Marketing

Partner agrees to:
- Feature integration in product UI/documentation
- Participate in co-marketing activities
- Maintain brand compliance per Hivemind guidelines
- Provide testimonials/case studies when requested

### 4.3 Support

Partner agrees to:
- Provide first-level support for integration questions
- Escalate technical issues to Hivemind within 24 hours
- Maintain response time SLA of 48 hours for customer inquiries

---

## 5. Hivemind Responsibilities

### 5.1 Technical Support

Hivemind agrees to:
- Provide comprehensive integration documentation
- Offer technical support via Slack/email
- Maintain API uptime of 99.9%
- Provide 30-day notice of breaking API changes

### 5.2 Marketing Support

Hivemind agrees to:
- Create co-branded marketing materials
- Feature Partner in Hivemind's partner directory
- Promote integration via Hivemind's marketing channels
- Provide Partner with referral tracking dashboard

### 5.3 Financial

Hivemind agrees to:
- Process commission payments on time
- Provide detailed monthly reports
- Maintain accurate referral tracking
- Resolve payment discrepancies within 10 business days

## 6. Brand Usage

### 6.1 Trademark License

Each party grants the other a non-exclusive, non-transferable license to use their trademarks, logos, and brand assets solely for:
- Marketing the partnership
- Integration documentation
- Co-branded content

### 6.2 Brand Guidelines

- Follow each other's brand guidelines
- Submit materials for approval before publication
- Use only approved logos and assets
- Maintain brand integrity and reputation

## 7. Data & Privacy

### 7.1 User Data

- Use customer data only for partnership purposes

### 7.2 Analytics

- Track integration usage and conversion metrics

## 8. Term & Termination

### 8.1 Term

This agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues for an initial term of **[INITIAL_TERM]** (e.g., 12 months), automatically renewing for successive [RENEWAL_TERM] periods unless terminated.

### 8.2 Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement:
- **With Cause:** Immediately upon material breach (30-day cure period)
- **Without Cause:** With 60 days written notice

### 8.3 Post-Termination

Upon termination:
- Integration may remain active for 90 days (wind-down period)
- Existing referrals continue earning commissions
- Final payment made within 30 days
- Both parties cease using each other's branding
