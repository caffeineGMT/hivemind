## Emergency Protocols

**If CAC exceeds $600**:
- Immediately pause underperforming campaigns
- Double down on organic channels
- Review and optimize landing page conversion rate

**If trial-to-paid conversion drops below 15%**:
- Audit onboarding flow
- Increase email touchpoints
- Add personal outreach for high-intent users

**If churn exceeds 5%**:
- Launch retention campaign
- Survey churned users
- Add features based on feedback
- Improve product education
